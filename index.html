What is Mind-OS?

Mind-OS is an open research framework designed to help you measure and understand your relationship with artificial intelligence.

It combines three validated-style instruments: the LLM-D12 dependency scale, the AIAS anxiety scale, and the AIRD burnout index.

All calculations happen in your browser. Your answers never leave your device unless you choose to participate in the anonymous global poll.

This is a self-reflection tool, not a clinical diagnosis.
