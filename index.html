🤖 AI: your tool or your companion? 3 answers that save your thinking

🧠 What AI really is?
AI is not a mind. It's a complex statistical calculator that finds patterns in huge data. It doesn't understand meaning, it computes probable answers.
⚙️ How to use AI wisely?
As a method, not a mind. Use it for drafts, facts, ideas. ALWAYS verify, rephrase, keep final decision for yourself.
⚠️ Why AI can be dangerous?
Risk of cognitive offloading. When answers come instantly, your brain stops memorizing, analyzing, deciding. You lose autonomy.
