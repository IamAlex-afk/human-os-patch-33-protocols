❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is cognitive offloading?▼
Cognitive offloading happens when you rely on external tools (like AI) to do mental tasks you could do yourself. Over time, this can weaken memory and independent thinking.
How does AI cause "brain fog"?▼
When you consistently outsource thinking to AI, the brain's neural pathways for problem-solving and memory retrieval become less active. This leads to mental sluggishness and difficulty concentrating without AI assistance.
What is AI anxiety (AIAS)?▼
AI anxiety is a modern form of technostress — fear of job loss, algorithmic anxiety, and obsessive concern about AI replacing human identity. The AI Anxiety Scale (AIAS, pooled α=0.949) measures this across four subscales.
What is AI Replacement Dysfunction (AIRD)?▼
AIRD is a condition where individuals experience significant distress and functional impairment due to the fear or perceived reality that AI technologies are replacing human roles, skills, and value. Symptoms include anxiety, insomnia, paranoia, and loss of identity.
Am I addicted to ChatGPT?▼
Addiction is a strong word, but unhealthy dependency is real. If you feel anxious without AI, constantly check for responses, or prefer AI over human interaction, it's a sign to rebalance. Our Anxiety & Emotional Attachment axis can help you assess this.
How are the test scores calculated?▼
The test uses a 5‑point Likert scale (0–4) across three axes: cognitive delegation, anxiety and emotional attachment, and digital burnout. Reverse‑keyed items control for social desirability bias. Each axis has 8 questions, for a maximum of 32 per axis and 96 overall.
Is this test anonymous?▼
Yes. All processing happens in your browser. Nothing is stored on any server. No signup required. The optional daily tracker saves data only in your device's localStorage.
Does using AI make me less intelligent?▼
It can, if you over‑rely on it. Cognitive offloading — using AI for memory and thinking — may weaken those skills over time. This test helps you gauge your current relationship with AI.
