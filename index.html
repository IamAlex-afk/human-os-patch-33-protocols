Free AI Dependency Tests

Answer honestly. Each test takes ~2 minutes. You'll get personalized insights and practical recommendations.

🧠 Test 1: Cognitive Autonomy Scale

Measures how much you rely on AI for memory, analysis, and decision-making. Based on Cognitive Offloading research (Nature, 2021).

💬 Test 2: Emotional Attachment to AI

Assesses emotional bonding with chatbots, discomfort when AI is unavailable, and replacement of human interaction.

⚙️ Test 3: Productivity & Decision-Making Scale

Evaluates AI dependency in work, writing, planning, and everyday choices. Identifies "paralysis by AI".

🤖 Why these tests matter

📉 Cognitive offloading
Overusing AI for thinking weakens neural pathways for memory and problem-solving.
🧠 Emotional dependency
AI companions can create attachment patterns similar to social media addiction.
⚡ Decision fatigue
Letting AI choose for you reduces your decision-making muscle over time.
